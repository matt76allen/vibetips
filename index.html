What Is Vibe Tips?

Vibe Tips is a simple, AI-powered tip calculator designed to make tipping feel better—for everyone involved. Whether your dining experience was just okay or absolutely exceptional, this app helps you reflect that in a more thoughtful way. You’ll receive a custom tip suggestion based on your answers to a few quick questions, along with a personalized thank-you message you can share online.

Unlike standard tip calculators, Vibe Tips guides you through an interactive process that takes into account your unique experience—things like friendliness, timing, attention to detail, and more. Your answers help shape both the suggested tip amount and a vibe-filled message of appreciation. It's like a digital high-five to the people who served you.

How It Works

Using Vibe Tips is fast and easy. Just enter the bill total, restaurant name, and a few other quick details. Our AI then generates custom vibe questions based on your input. You respond using sliders, and the app calculates a recommended tip that reflects your overall experience. Finally, we generate a personalized thank-you message that you can copy and share on social media—whether it’s Instagram, Facebook, X (Twitter), or wherever you like to post.

Every time you use Vibe Tips, you're helping spark a little more kindness, public recognition, and good energy for the people behind the scenes. Whether you share your message or just use the tool privately, it's a small way to make gratitude part of the experience.
